INTERMEDIATE+ SPEAKING SKILLS

All intermediate and advanced English students want to improve their speaking skills. Here is a list of ESL questions to help them do just that, inspired by Vogue's fantastic 73 Questions series. Don't worry if they don't have all the answers, or if you find yourself asking some of them in rapid succession, just like in the videos. Ask follow-up questions to keep them talking. For pairwork, you can download the English speaking skills practise as a printable worksheet here.

    73 Intermediate Deep Speaking Questions


  • 1. How do you feel right now?
  • 2. What’s your favourite time of day?
  • 3. Would you like to be famous?
  • 4. What makes you angry?
  • 5. What makes you happy?
  • 6. Who makes you laugh?
  • 7. Books or films?
  • 8. What’s your favourite film/tv show?
  • 9. What are you binge-watching right now?
  • 10. What’s your favourite holiday destination?
  • 11. What’s your perfect day?
  • 12. What’s your biggest strength?
  • 13. Sweet or savoury?
  • 14. What song can you listen to again and again?
  • 15. Who are you most like in your family?
  • 16. What cause do you support?
  • 17. What’s one thing people don’t know about you?
  • 18. Vintage or new?
  • 19. What’s your favourite board game?
  • 20. What’s your favourite computer/mobile game?
  • 21. What do you sing in the shower?
  • 22. What would your super-power be?
  • 23. What did you want to be when you were small?
  • 24. Bike, car or horse?
  • 25. What’s your favourite food?
  • 26. What’s your favourite drink?
  • 27. How many pairs of shoes have you got?
  • 28. Window seat or aisle?
  • 29. What’s an adventurous thing you’ve done?
  • 30. What’s been the best year of your life so far?
  • 31. What’s your favourite item of clothing?
  • 32. What’s the best piece of advice you’ve received?
  • 33. If you could go back in time for a day, when would you go to?
  • 34. What’s one thing on your bucket list?
  • 35. What’s one of your earliest memories?
  • 36. What are three positive things about you?
  • 37. When did you last laugh?
  • 38. When did you last cry?
  • 39. Mountain or beach?
  • 40. Do you have a tattoo?
  • If so, how did you come to have it?
  • 41. What do you think of tattoos?
  • 42. Are you usually early or often late?
  • 43. A night person or a morning person?
  • 44. What quirk do you have?
  • 45. What are you currently reading?
  • 46. What’s your favourite book?
  • 47. What’s one place you’ve always dreamed of going?
  • 48. Which famous person would you like to have lunch with?
  • 49. What are the three things you do most on your phone?
  • 50. What’s a talent you wish you had?
  • 51. What’s your favourite sport/exercise?
  • 52. What would you like to learn?
  • 53. What’s your favourite band?
  • 54. What’s your favourite fast food?
  • 55. What’s your favourite colour?
  • 56. Cats or dogs?
  • 57. Wisdom or youth?
  • 58. How much time do you spend online?
  • 59. What is one of your ambitions?
  • 60. What goal did you achieve last year?
  • 61. What job would you be terrible at?
  • 62. Who or what cheers you up when you are down?
  • 63. Looks or intelligence?
  • 64. What makes you mad?
  • 65. How often do you buy clothes?
  • 66. Are you tidy or disorganised?
  • 67. How long does it take you to get ready in the morning?
  • 68. Do you love or hate rollercoasters?
  • 69. What animal would you be?
  • 70. Who knows you the best?
  • 71. Are you a talker or a listener?
  • 72. Do you like surprises?
  • 73. What's one question I've missed?
